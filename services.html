Our Services

Everything you need for modern healthcare management in Nigeria.

NIN-Based Patient Records

Every patient is identified by their 11-digit National Identification Number. This creates a permanent, unforgeable health identity that follows the patient across all hospitals and clinics in Nigeria.

  • No duplicate records
  • Instant record retrieval
  • Secure SHA-256 hashing
  • Cross-hospital compatibility

Find Care

Search for medical professionals near you with GPS-based proximity search. Filter by profession, radius, and availability. View full profiles with contact info, office hours, and directions.

  • GPS proximity search
  • 100+ profession filters
  • "Open Now" indicator
  • Direct calling & Google Maps

Hospital Dashboard

Complete patient management for hospitals. Create new records, search existing patients by NIN, add medical remarks, and track patient history — all in one place.

  • Create patient records
  • NIN-only search (secure)
  • Add medical remarks
  • View full history

Medical Worker Access

Medical workers get secure, read-only access to patient records. Search by NIN, view medical history and remarks, but never modify — maintaining data integrity and patient safety.

  • Read-only patient access
  • NIN-based search
  • View remarks & history
  • Professional profile

Secure Authentication

Multiple layers of security: password login, OTP email verification, JWT token-based sessions, and Brevo-powered email delivery. Your data is protected at every step.

  • Password + OTP login
  • JWT token sessions
  • Brevo email integration
  • Password reset

Progressive Web App

Install Thrivelink on your phone like a native app. Works offline, responsive design, push notifications ready, and feels like a real product — not a website.

  • Install on home screen
  • Offline capable
  • Push notifications
  • Native app feel
