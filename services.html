What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive health solutions for individuals, organizations, and institutions across Nigeria.

Health Consultancy & Advisory

Wellness programs, medical emergency solutions, health education, and corporate health talks tailored to your organization's specific needs. We help businesses and institutions build healthier workplaces.

Medical Supplies & Consumables

Distribution of quality health products, PPE, and medical equipment to hospitals, clinics, and organizations nationwide. We ensure reliable supply chains for critical healthcare materials.

Health Screenings & Outreach

Community health checks, workplace wellness programs, and preventive health campaigns. We bring healthcare to the people who need it most, right where they are.

Linkages & Referrals

Connect clients to certified hospitals, diagnostic labs, and specialist practitioners through our verified provider network. We make sure you get the right care at the right place.

Procurement Support

Enlistment and supply to institutions, NGOs, and government bodies with transparent procurement processes. We help organizations access quality health products at competitive rates.

Emergency Response Coordination

Rapid connection to nearest available hospitals and health workers during medical emergencies, powered by real-time location data. Every second counts in an emergency.

Our Platform

The Thrivelink Platform

More than a service company — we are building the infrastructure for connected healthcare in Nigeria

For Hospitals & Clinics

Create patient records, search cross-facility histories by NIN, and add append-only clinical remarks. Continuity of care, finally solved.

For Patients

No account creation needed. Access your medical history via OTP using the phone or email your hospital has on file. View records, update contact details securely.

For Health Workers

Register with your medical license. Get verified and appear in emergency provider searches. Be found when patients need you most.

Privacy by Design

Raw NINs are never stored. All identifiers are hashed. Clinical data is append-only and immutable. NDPR compliant from day one.

Emergency Access

Find the nearest hospital or verified health worker in seconds. Location-aware search with platform-registered providers prioritized.
