About Thrivelink

Building the future of healthcare in Nigeria, one record at a time.

Our Mission

To bridge the healthcare gap in Nigeria by creating a secure, accessible, and unified national health record system that empowers hospitals, medical workers, and patients with instant access to critical health information — anywhere, anytime.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where no patient dies because their medical history is unknown. Where every hospital, clinic, and medical professional can access the full picture of a patient's health within seconds — powered by secure NIN-based identification.

The Problem We Solve

Why Thrivelink Exists

01

Fragmented Records

Patient records are scattered across different hospitals, clinics, and paper files. When a patient moves or visits a new facility, their history is lost.

02

Emergency Delays

In emergencies, doctors waste precious minutes trying to understand a patient's allergies, blood type, and medical history. Every second counts.

03

No Central Directory

Finding a nearby doctor, pharmacist, or midwife is difficult. Patients rely on word-of-mouth or travel long distances for basic care.

04

Our Solution

Thrivelink unifies health records under a single NIN, creates a searchable directory of all medical professionals, and makes healthcare accessible to every Nigerian.
