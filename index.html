Our Platform

The Thrivelink Platform

More than a service company — we are building the infrastructure for connected healthcare in Nigeria

For Hospitals & Clinics

Create patient records, search cross-facility histories by NIN, and add append-only clinical remarks.

For Patients

No account creation needed. Access your medical history via OTP. View records, update contact details securely.

For Health Workers

Register with your medical license. Get verified and appear in emergency provider searches. Read-only patient access.

Privacy by Design

Raw NINs are never stored. All identifiers are hashed. Clinical data is append-only and immutable. NDPR compliant.

Emergency Access

Find the nearest hospital or verified health worker in seconds. Location-aware search with platform-registered providers prioritized.
