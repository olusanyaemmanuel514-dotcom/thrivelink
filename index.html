Our Platform

The Thrivelink Platform

More than a service company — we are building the infrastructure for connected healthcare in Nigeria

For Hospitals & Clinics

Create patient records, search cross-facility histories by NIN, and add append-only clinical remarks. Continuity of care, finally solved.

For Patients

No account creation needed. Access your medical history via OTP. View records, update contact details securely.

For Health Workers

Register with your medical license. Get verified and appear in emergency provider searches. Be found when patients need you.

Privacy by Design

Raw NINs are never stored. All identifiers are hashed. Clinical data is append-only and immutable. NDPR compliant.
