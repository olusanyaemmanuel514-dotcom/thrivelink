What We Offer

Built for Every Healthcare Need

NIN-Based Records

Every patient gets a secure, permanent health record tied to their National Identification Number. No duplicates, no lost files.

Find Care Near You

Search for doctors, pharmacists, midwives, dentists, and all medical professionals within any radius. Filter by profession and availability.

Hospital Dashboard

Full patient management for hospitals: create records, add medical remarks, track history, and update your facility information.
